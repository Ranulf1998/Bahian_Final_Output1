FAVORITE LESSON

Among all the lessons this semester, my favorite is the HTML/CSS where we learned to make our own website. It's pretty interesting to me knowing how people make a website. It's hard at first but I'm slowly learning, making my own website is pretty fun.

What makes HTML/CSS my favorite lesson extends beyond the technicalities. It's the sense of enjoyment that comes with creating something tangible, something that the world can interact with. From the first line of code that declares the doctype to the last stylized flourish that breathes life into a button, each keystroke is a brushstroke in the digital artistry of web design.

Website making isn't just a lesson for me but a gateway to a world where creativity and technology converge. The joy of turning the blank canvas into something beautiful is a piece of art, the satisfaction you can get after finishing without errors is irreplaceable. There's still a lot to learn and the journey is long but this is the start of something else.
